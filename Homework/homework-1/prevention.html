Virus Prevention: Can be simple by following preventative steps regularly.

  1. - Have some type of Anti-Virus software.
  2. - Keep your Anti-Virus software up to date.
  3. - Update the virus definitions on your computer almost every week. To make sure your coverage is up to date.
  4. - Scan your computer for viruses every 7-10 days. This way you can eliminate the virus immediatly.
