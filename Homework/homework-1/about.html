Bellow is a list of some of the types of viruses on the internet today. These each damage/attack in their own particular way and target specifit peices of software or code on their host.

Computer Virus» Replicates and attaches itself to programs or files. It can then secretly activate and execute files; Or even erase disks and attach multiple files together.

Worms» Self-replicating peices of code that dont need to attach to the host. They can spread and execute themselves, aka their code, damaging the system the are in.

Trojan Horses» A non replicating program that appears to be a part of the system, but then attacks the system when exicuted. This can be used to steal passwords and user information or perminatly damage data on the hard disk.

Spyware & Adware» Software that collects and sends information to a third party without the users knowlage or consent.
